# Associate the customer with other entities

> **POST** /customers/_\{uuid}_/entities

The association of a customer with other entities, allows the record of the customer to be available in the intended entities.

To perform this association it is necessary to know the _uuid_ of the customer and the _clientId_ of the entities.

**1.** Associate the customer with the _uuid_ e2343605-cf46-43de-b20b-9b7d1c95a9b2 and the entity with the _clientId_ 523308191 e 559514166
